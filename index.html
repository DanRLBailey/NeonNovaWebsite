Art Style

The art style is one of the main draws to the game, being heavily inspired by neon and futurism. The game is very vibrant and colourful and is guaranteed to draw your attention.

Music Style

The loud, bassy, techno music was chosen to fit with the games futuristic aesthetic. It also gives the game a sense of speed and urgency and will cause you to keep wanting to move forward.
